Mingming's Adoption Story

Mingming was rescued from the streets of Manila. She was malnourished and needed immediate care. After weeks of rehabilitation at our shelter, she found a loving home with the Dela Cruz family. Now, she spends her days napping in sunny spots and playing with her favorite yarn ball.

×

Muning's Adoption Story

Muning was found after Typhoon Odette, scared and alone. He was brought to our shelter where he slowly regained trust in humans. The Santos family opened their hearts to Muning, giving him a forever home where he's cherished and adored.

×

Lucky's Adoption Story

Lucky was found wandering the streets of Cebu, weak and hungry. After being nursed back to health, she was adopted by the Garcia family. She now enjoys playing fetch and bringing joy to her new family every day.

×

Millie's Adoption Story

Millie was abandoned in a local park and found by one of our volunteers. She was shy and scared at first but soon warmed up to the staff and other pets at the shelter. After a few months, she found her forever home with the Johnson family, where she now spends her days playing with her new siblings and enjoying long walks.
