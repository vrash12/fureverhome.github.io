Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) for newly adopted furiends

1 Can I adopt if I live in an apartment or small space? +

Yes, many pets are suitable for apartment living. It's important to choose a pet that matches your living space and lifestyle. Consider adopting smaller breeds or pets that require less space and exercise.

2 What should I prepare before bringing my new pet home? +

Before bringing your new pet home, ensure you have all the necessary supplies such as food, bowls, a bed, toys, and any other essentials. It's also important to pet-proof your home to keep them safe.

3 How do I introduce my new pet to existing pets in the household? +

Introducing a new pet should be done gradually. Start by keeping them separated and allow them to get used to each other's scents. Slowly introduce them in a controlled environment and supervise their interactions.
