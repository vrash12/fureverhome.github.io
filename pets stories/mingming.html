Mingming's Adoption Story

2 years old | Puspin (Pusang Pinoy)

Mingming was found wandering the streets as a playful kitten, looking for warmth and a place to belong. Our team quickly took her in, and she showed a lively and affectionate personality from day one. The Santos family saw her playful antics and gentle nature, and they knew Mingming was the perfect addition to their home. Mingming now enjoys sunny afternoons lounging by the window and playful evenings with her new family.

"Mingming has brought so much joy and laughter into our lives. Her playful spirit and loving nature make every day brighter."

- Maria Santos
