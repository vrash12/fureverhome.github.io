Muning's Adoption Story

3 years old | Orange Tabby

Muning was found wandering alone in the busy streets of Cebu. She was timid and scared, hiding behind cars and dumpsters. Our team provided her with the necessary medical care and a safe environment to heal. After several weeks of trust-building and rehabilitation, the Garcia family opened their hearts and home to Muning. Today, she enjoys lounging on window sills and chasing butterflies in the garden.

"Muning has brought so much happiness into our lives. Her playful nature and affectionate demeanor make every day brighter."

- Juan Garcia
