Lucky's Adoption Story

2 years old | Aspin (Asong Pinoy)

Lucky was surrendered to our shelter after her previous owner could no longer care for her. As a resilient Aspin, Lucky adapted quickly to her new surroundings, showing affection and loyalty to everyone she met. The Reyes family was captivated by her gentle nature and decided to give her a permanent home. Lucky now enjoys cozy evenings by the fireplace and playful afternoons in the backyard.

"Lucky has been a blessing to our family. Her loving personality and playful antics bring so much joy into our home."

- Roland Reyes
