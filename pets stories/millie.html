Millie's Adoption Story

2 years old | American Boxer

Millie was brought to our shelter after her previous owner had to relocate for work. As an energetic American Boxer, Millie thrived in our spacious play areas and quickly bonded with our staff. The Thompson family fell in love with her playful spirit and decided to make Millie a part of their lives. Now, Millie enjoys daily walks in the park and playful sessions with her new family.

"Millie has been a wonderful addition to our family. Her energy and love keep us active and happy every day."

- Steve Thompson
