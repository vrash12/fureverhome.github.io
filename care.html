Caring for Your New Pet

Feeding

Correct Feeding Practices

  • Twice a Day: Feed your dog morning and evening.
  • Proper Diet: Offer dog food or locally available food like boiled meat, rice, and vegetables.

Bad Feeding Practices

  • Avoid Leftovers: Especially with bones, which can cause choking.
  • No Sweets: Chocolate and sugary treats are toxic to dogs.

Correct Feeding Practices

  • Regular Meals: Feed your cat dry or wet food, or a mix of fish and rice twice a day.
  • Clean Water: Ensure a constant supply of clean drinking water.

Bad Feeding Practices

  • No Dairy: Despite popular belief, milk can upset a cat's stomach.
  • Toxic Foods: Avoid onions, garlic, chocolate, and caffeine, as they can be harmful.
